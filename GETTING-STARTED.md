## Glossary
- **Aesthetic thesis**: the core visual/tonal direction.
- **Signature differentiator**: one instantly recognizable identity anchor.
- **Token**: reusable variable for color/type/space/motion/surface.
- **Primitive**: reusable base component (button/input/card).
- **Composition**: how primitives combine into sections and flows.
